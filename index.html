Section 1

There are no heroes in a punk rock band. Karen realized the only way she was getting into heaven was to cheat. While on the first date he accidentally hit his head on the beam. He dreamed of leaving his law firm to open a portable dog wash. You realize you're not alone as you sit in your bedroom massaging your calves after a long day of playing tug-of-war with Grandpa Joe in the hospital. Peanuts don't grow on trees, but cashews do. He wasn't bitter that she had moved on but from the radish. He learned the hardest lesson of his life and had the scars, both physical and mental, to prove it. Boulders lined the side of the road foretelling what could come next. He found the chocolate covered roaches quite tasty.
